# Upwork Jobs Analysis: Bioinformatics & Computational Biology

## Summary

This document provides an analysis and examples of Upwork job postings in the fields of bioinformatics, computational biology, and protein engineering. These positions typically require advanced expertise in biological data analysis, machine learning, and research collaboration.

### Key Trends

- **Project Types**: Research collaborations, data analysis, algorithm development, academic writing
- **Duration**: Typically 1-6 months, part-time (less than 30 hrs/week)
- **Compensation**: $4-$120/hour for expert-level positions (varies by project type, freelancer experience, and geographic location)
- **Skills in Demand**: Bioinformatics, Python/R programming, Machine Learning/AI, Academic Writing, Computational Chemistry
- **Client Profile**: Research institutions, biotech companies, academic researchers
- **Freelancer Diversity**: Wide range from Top Rated Plus ($120/hr) to Top Rated from lower-cost regions ($4-$35/hr), all with PhD-level expertise
